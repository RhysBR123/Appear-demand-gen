Recommendations

Six things to get right
before September.

Based on our experience running demand gen for technology brands, and what we know about where Appear is today, here's where we'd focus first.

Recommendations · 1 of 6
01

Refresh your Book-of-Business lists by region

Work with Sales leaders to update BoB lists across North America (including Canada), LATAM, EMEA, and APAC. Clean, current lists are the foundation of any targeted demand gen programme. Without them, you're spending budget on the wrong people.

Recommendations · 2 of 6
02

Lock in your ICP — then go after lookalikes

Once you've agreed your Ideal Customer Profile, use it to identify lookalike companies and run always-on brand awareness campaigns against them. This creates a constant stream of new opportunities — companies your Sales team may not have on their radar yet — across all global regions.

Worth noting

Knowing who you're targeting is half the equation. Knowing where to reach them is the rest. LinkedIn is the natural home for Appear's target audience, but the most successful demand gen campaigns don't rely on a single channel. Conduct broader channel research to find your prospects elsewhere too — you may be surprised where they're showing up.

Recommendations · 3 of 6
03

Define your top personas and tailor everything

Agree on the two or three most critical personas — for example, Operations vs. Engineering — and tailor ad creative, landing pages, and nurture emails to each. One-size-fits-all messaging doesn't convert.

Recommendations · 4 of 6
04

Audit what you already have

Before building new content, take stock of what exists. A full content audit across campaigns, sales assets, videos, and collateral will surface material that can be repurposed, refreshed, or repackaged — saving budget and accelerating activation. You may already have more to work with than you think.

Recommendations · 5 of 6
05

Run always-on brand awareness — not just seasonal

Seasonal campaigns around events like NAB are important, but they can't carry the full weight of a demand gen programme. Always-on brand awareness ads running year-round build a steady stream of top-of-funnel prospects, feed retargeting pools ahead of seasonal pushes, and maintain momentum through quieter periods — including Q5 (December–January).

Recommendations · 6 of 6
06

Build a campaign playbook for Sales

A simple one-pager per campaign showing which ads are running, what the key messages are, and suggested follow-up templates. It makes the Marketing-to-Sales handoff seamless and means Sales can follow up with confidence from day one.

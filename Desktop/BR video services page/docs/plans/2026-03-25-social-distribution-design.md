# Social & Distribution Page Design
**Date:** 2026-03-25
**File to create:** `social-distribution.html`

## Overview
A service page for Branch Road's Social & Distribution offering. Three service sections following the same design language as `demand-generation.html` — no visual contrast variation, consistent dark background throughout. Problems baked into copy, not stated explicitly.

## Core Tensions (woven into copy)
1. Organic and paid running in silos — not connected to a shared narrative
2. Reporting that explains what happened but not what to do next

## Branch Road Differentiator
Organic and paid built around the same narrative. Tracking that informs decisions, not just justifies spend.

## Hero
- **Eyebrow:** Social & Distribution
- **Headline:** Social and paid that pull in the same direction
- **Sub-copy:** Most B2B social generates likes from competitors and reports that explain what happened. We connect organic and paid to a single narrative, track what matters, and tell you what to do next.
- **Right side:** Placeholder interactive card

## Section Structure

### Section 2 — Organic social
- **Headline:** A presence worth following
- **Copy:** Organic social that exists to fill a calendar doesn't build pipeline. We build a presence around a clear point of view — content that reaches the right people and gives them a reason to engage.
- **Output pills:** Content strategy · LinkedIn presence · Executive social · Community management · Social copywriting · Content calendar
- **Example card title:** LinkedIn content programme
- **Example card body:** A consistent, POV-driven presence built around your narrative, not just company updates. Content that earns attention from buyers, not just peers.
- **Example card tags:** POV-driven · Executive-led · Pipeline-focused

### Section 3 — Paid social & PPC
- **Headline:** Spend that earns its place
- **Copy:** Paid without a narrative is just noise with a budget. We run campaigns built around the same story as your organic — targeted at the right audience, at the right stage, with creative that doesn't look like an ad.
- **Output pills:** LinkedIn ads · Paid social · PPC · Audience targeting · Ad creative · A/B testing · Retargeting
- **Example card title:** Demand gen campaign
- **Example card body:** A paid programme tied directly to your content narrative, not running parallel to it. Same message, right audience, right stage.
- **Example card tags:** Narrative-aligned · Stage-targeted · Creative-led

### Section 4 — Campaign tracking & performance reporting
- **Headline:** Measurement that tells you what to do next
- **Copy:** Most reporting tells you what happened. We build tracking frameworks that tell you why — and what to change. Attribution that connects spend to pipeline, not just clicks.
- **Output pills:** Campaign tracking · UTM framework · Attribution modelling · Dashboard setup · Performance reporting · Channel analysis
- **Example card title:** Performance dashboard
- **Example card body:** A live view of what's working across organic and paid, built to inform decisions not justify spend. Connected to pipeline, not just impressions.
- **Example card tags:** Pipeline-connected · Cross-channel · Decision-ready

### Section 5 — CTA
- **Headline:** Ready to make your distribution work harder?
- **Sub-copy:** Tell us what you're running. We'll show you what's missing.
- Contact link + back to services link

## Design Language
- Identical CSS, nav, hero, CTA, and JS to `demand-generation.html`
- Three sections with alternating layout: copy left/visual right → flipped → copy left/visual right
- Same output pill and service visual card treatment throughout
- No light section — all dark background

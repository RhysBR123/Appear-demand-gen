# Demand Generation Page Design
**Date:** 2026-03-25
**File to create:** `demand-generation.html`

## Overview
A service page for Branch Road's Demand Generation offering. Follows the design language of `index.html` and `explainer-video-production.html` but with its own structure — four service area sections rather than a format showcase grid. No separate "problem" section; the tensions (boring B2B content, lack of specificity and POV) are baked into the copy.

## Core Tensions (woven into copy, not stated explicitly)
1. Most B2B content is boring
2. Most B2B content doesn't resonate — because it's bland, not targeted at the right level, and lacks a real POV or narrative

## Branch Road Differentiator
- Different content formats (not just another PDF ebook)
- "Altitude" thinking — strategy and targeting before content production
- Avoid scaring away readers who don't want the strategy piece; lead with outputs and let the approach speak for itself

---

## Page Structure

### Section 1 — Hero
- **Eyebrow:** Demand Generation
- **Headline:** B2B content that moves buyers
- **Sub-copy:** Most B2B content gets ignored. Not because there isn't enough of it — because it doesn't say anything. We make content that earns attention, takes a real position, and reaches the right people at the right level.
- **Right side:** Placeholder interactive card (labelled for future tool)

### Section 2 — Narrative-led content & interactive assets
- **Headline:** Content with a spine
- **Copy:** Most content tries to cover everything. We make content built around a clear argument — formats that hold attention and give buyers something to do, share, or act on.
- **Output cards:** Interactive assessments · Long-form narratives · Research reports · Campaign landing pages · Content hubs

### Section 3 — Thought leadership
- **Headline:** Ideas worth being associated with
- **Copy:** Thought leadership only works if there's an actual thought in it. We develop POV-driven content that makes your buyers want to share it with their peers.
- **Output cards:** Point of view series · Executive briefings · Industry research · Bylined articles · Newsletter content

### Section 4 — Sales enablement
- **Headline:** Content that works in the room
- **Copy:** Most sales content sits in a shared drive. We build materials that actually get used — at the right stage, with the right message, for the right buyer.
- **Output cards:** Battlecards · One-pagers · Pitch decks · Case study frameworks · Objection handling guides

### Section 5 — Customer advocacy
- **Headline:** Let your customers do the talking
- **Copy:** Peer trust beats brand trust. We turn customer stories into assets that work across the entire funnel — not just a PDF on the website.
- **Output cards:** Customer stories · Video testimonials · Case studies · Reference programs · ROI narratives

### Section 6 — CTA
- Simple, direct closing section
- Copy direction: "Ready to make content that actually lands?"
- Contact / calendar link

---

## Design Language
- Match visual style of `index.html` and `explainer-video-production.html`
- Each service section: bold section headline + 2–3 line copy block + row of small output-type cards/pills below
- Alternating or consistent layout per section (TBD during build)
- Placeholder card in hero styled consistently with the page
